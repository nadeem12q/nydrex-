Privacy Policy.

Nydrex Plus collects information you choose to share through the contact form, WhatsApp, or email, such as name, email, phone, service needed, budget range, timeline, and project message.

This information is used to respond to inquiries, discuss project requirements, prepare estimates, and provide support. Nydrex Plus does not sell personal data.

If analytics or cookies are added, they should be used only for basic website performance and improvement. You can request correction or deletion of inquiry data by contacting Nydrex Plus.
